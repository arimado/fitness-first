Welcome

At Fitness First, we’re making changes for the better. To ensure that we remain Australia’s leading fitness provider, we’re committed to implementing new work conditions that will be attractive to the best people in the industry – including those who already work here, like you.

We are proposing a new Enterprise Agreement under which all full-time, part-time and casual employees will be better off – not just financially, but also in terms of working conditions. We’re proud to put forward a series of positive changes – such as paid overtime, annual salary increases, and paid parental leave – that are not only fair, but in many cases will also be the best offered in the industry.

Click here to download the summary of proposed changes to wages and working conditions under the draft Enterprise Agreement.

It’s important that everyone understands exactly how the proposed Agreement will benefit them. This website aims to communicate all of the important changes and conditions in a way that is clear and easy to understand. We don’t want there to be any uncertainty or lack of clarity. Everyone is encouraged to attend an information sessions and to submit any questions to the HR team via the FAQ section.

On 25th September 2013, we are entering into an Enterprise Bargaining process with all employees. This is a chance for you to have your say about the proposed changes, either by nominating yourself as Bargaining Representative and/or voting on whether you would like the Agreement implemented. The Enterprise Bargaining process is explained in full here.

Once the bargaining committee has agreed to the terms under the proposed Agreement, we will make the complete document available so that you can read it thoroughly and ask questions before voting.
